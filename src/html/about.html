About

Welcome to Bookmark! I’m Tori, a mother of four who is obsessed with children's books. J.K. Rowling said, "If you don't like to read, you haven't found the right book." I believe that's true, and I think there is a book (or two or ten) out there that every child can connect with. I created Bookmark to help kids find those books and bring the joy and ease back into reading.

Check Bookmark out on Instagram to see book reviews, plus reading tools and tricks.

Follow along on Bookroo to see more reviews, book lists, and our current reads and favorite books.

Speaking of J.K. Rowling, you can download our free Harry Potter School Guide, a 20-page guide covering books one through three that is filled with learning activities that cover a variety of subjects!
